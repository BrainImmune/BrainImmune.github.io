## Open Positions

### Postdoctoral Fellows

We are seeking postdoctoral researchers with a background in one or more of the following areas:

- Epigenomics, chromatin biology, or gene regulation
- Single-cell genomics (scATAC-seq, scRNA-seq, or multiomics)
- Computational biology, bioinformatics, or machine learning
- Neuroscience or neuroimmunology

**What we offer:** A stimulating and collaborative research environment, access to cutting-edge technologies and datasets, mentorship for career development (academic or industry), and competitive compensation.

**To apply:** Please send an email to [lab email — *please update*] with the subject line "Postdoc Application — [Your Name]" and include:
1. A brief cover letter describing your research background and why you are interested in the lab
2. Your CV
3. Contact information for 2–3 references

### Undergraduate Researchers

We occasionally take undergraduate students for research projects, particularly those with strong quantitative backgrounds or interest in wet lab genomics techniques.

**Requirements:**
- Commitment of at least 10 hours/week for a minimum of one semester
- Strong interest in genomics, neuroscience, or computational biology
- Prior coursework in biology, genetics, or programming is a plus

**To apply:** Send a CV and a brief description of your research interests to [lab email].

## What We Look For

Regardless of position, we value:

- **Intellectual curiosity** — genuine interest in understanding biology
- **Rigor** — attention to detail and commitment to reproducible science
- **Collaboration** — a team player who communicates openly
- **Resilience** — science is hard; we support each other through challenges
- **Diversity** — we believe diverse perspectives make better science

We are committed to fostering an inclusive and equitable environment for all lab members.
