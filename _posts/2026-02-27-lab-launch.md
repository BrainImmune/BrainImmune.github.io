Welcome to the Bridge-AD Lab website! We are excited to officially launch our online presence.

Our lab is dedicated to understanding the epigenomic mechanisms underlying Alzheimer's Disease, with a particular focus on brain and immune cell types. Using single-cell technologies including scATAC-seq, scRNA-seq, and spatial transcriptomics, we aim to decode the regulatory landscape of AD at unprecedented resolution.

We are currently recruiting **postdocs**, **graduate students**, and **undergraduate researchers**. If you are interested in joining, please visit our [Join Us](/join/) page or [Contact](/contact/) us directly.

Stay tuned for updates on our research, publications, and lab news!
