Chimeric antigen receptor (CAR)-T immunotherapy has yielded impressive results in several B cell malignancies. A major outstanding question is whether CAR-T integration enhances the proliferative competence of individual T cells by rewiring their regulatory landscape. We developed EpiVIA ([GitHub](https://github.com/VahediLab/epiVIA)) for the joint profiling of chromatin accessibility and lentiviral integration sites at population and single-cell levels. Our findings reveal how CAR-T vector integration into specific genomic loci can influence local chromatin accessibility and T cell function.
