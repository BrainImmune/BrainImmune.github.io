Chimeric antigen receptor (CAR)-T immunotherapy has yielded impressive results in several B cell malignancies. A major outstanding question is whether CAR-T integration enhances the proliferative competence of individual T cells by rewiring their regulatory landscape. We developed **EpiVIA** ([GitHub](https://github.com/VahediLab/epiVIA)) for the joint profiling of chromatin accessibility and lentiviral integration sites at population and single-cell levels, enabling single-cell deconstruction of gene regulation during CAR-T therapy.
